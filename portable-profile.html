※ A guided builder, not another chatbot

Seven sections. Eight to twelve minutes.
One profile that travels with you.

We walk you through who you are, how you communicate, the workflows you do every week, the people and projects that come up again and again, the things you don't want, and the goals worth keeping in view.

01 · Identity

Who you are

Name, role, field, and a short paragraph that captures the work you do.

02 · Style

How it should talk to you

Tone, length, formatting, whether to ask before assuming.

03 · Workflows

What you use AI for

The recurring tasks you've done more than once.

04 · Context

Your recurring world

Projects, key people, terms and acronyms you use.

05 · Boundaries

What to avoid

Topics to skip, phrases that grate, suggestions that miss.

06 · Goals

The bigger picture

What you're working toward, so suggestions land sharper.

07 · Export

Take it anywhere

Universal markdown, ChatGPT custom instructions, or first-message paste.
